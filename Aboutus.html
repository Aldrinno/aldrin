Who We Are

History

Recidencia Del Hamor in Casiguran, Sorsogon is a serene retreat that blends luxury and nature. Inspired by the local word "Hamor", meaning love and kindness, it offers a peaceful escape where elegance meets heartfelt hospitality. Each stay is a reflection of Casiguran’s charm — a place where every guest becomes part of its story.

Our Commitment to Serve

At Recidencia Del Hamor, we are dedicated to providing exceptional hospitality rooted in warmth, comfort, and care. Our mission is to create a serene haven where every guest feels valued — ensuring each stay is as memorable as the landscapes that surround us.

Policies and Procedures

At Recidencia Del Hamor, we aim to provide a seamless and relaxing experience for all our guests. Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours prior to arrival. We accept cash, credit cards, and online transfers for payments. Smoking is only allowed in designated areas, and pets are not permitted, except for service animals. For any inquiries, please don’t hesitate to contact us — your comfort is our priority.
